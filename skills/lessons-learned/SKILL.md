Follow the method in [extracting-lessons.md](extracting-lessons.md) — it
stays canonical there. In short: mine the session for misunderstandings, suboptimal actions,
and measured wall-time waste; dedupe ruthlessly against the whole corpus; convert each keeper
down the promotion ladder (setting → hook → check → skill → prose — see
[checks/DESIGN.md](../../checks/DESIGN.md)); "no new lessons" is the common, valid outcome.
Deliver any capture as a PR for review, never straight to `main`.
